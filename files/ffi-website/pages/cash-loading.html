Service Overview

ATM Cash Loading — What We Offer

Ford Frontier Investments provides professional cash loading services for ATM operators across our Alabama service area. We work with independent ATM owners, operators in our Partnership Program, and businesses who want the peace of mind of having a reliable, local loader on call.

How It Works

  1. We confirm your ATM's location and loading requirements
  2. We set up a cash loading schedule based on your transaction volume
  3. Our insured loader visits on schedule and fills your machine
  4. You receive visit confirmation after every loading
  5. We alert you if the machine reaches low-cash thresholds between visits
Pricing

Simple, Transparent Pricing

Our cash loading service is priced at $1 per transaction processed during the period we're actively loading your machine. No hidden fees, no setup costs, no minimums.

For high-volume locations, armored carrier cash replenishment services may be available. Contact us to discuss your volume and we'll recommend the right solution.

Don't Own an ATM Yet?

Our free ATM placement program handles all cash loading for you at no charge. If you want an ATM without any loading responsibility, that's your best option.

FAQ

Common Questions About Cash Loading

How is cash loading priced?

$1 per transaction processed while we're actively loading. No setup fees, no minimums, no surprises.

What if my ATM runs out of cash between visits?

We monitor cash levels and alert you when the machine approaches a low threshold. For high-volume locations, we can increase loading frequency.

Do I need to own the ATM to use this service?

Yes — this service is for ATM owners and operators. If you don't own an ATM, our free placement program handles all loading at no cost.

Can I use cash loading with the ATM Partnership Program?

Yes. If you're enrolled in the Partnership Program but prefer not to load yourself, we can arrange loading on your behalf. Ask us about combining programs.

Is cash loading available statewide?

We serve Birmingham, Montgomery, and Tuscaloosa as our primary markets, with ongoing expansion. Contact us if you're outside these areas.
