Acquisition Details

We Buy ATM Routes in Alabama

If you own ATM locations in Alabama and are looking to sell — whether you're retiring, scaling back, relocating, or simply ready to move on — Ford Frontier Investments is an active buyer of ATM portfolios in our market.

We buy all sizes. There's no minimum number of machines required. Whether you're selling one ATM location or a network of twenty, we'll evaluate what you have and make a fair, straightforward offer.

What We Typically Look For

  • ATMs located in Alabama (our primary acquisition market)
  • Machines with active location agreements in place
  • Consistent transaction history (lower-volume locations still considered)
  • Hyosung and Genmega equipment preferred — other brands evaluated case by case
  • EMV-compliant machines in working condition

The Process

  • Step 1: Contact us with your portfolio details (machines, locations, monthly transaction volume)
  • Step 2: We review the information and schedule a call to discuss
  • Step 3: We make a fair written offer based on transaction history and location quality
  • Step 4: If you accept, we handle all transition details — equipment transfer, location agreement assignments, processing changeover

Confidential Inquiries Welcome

We understand that not everyone wants to publicize that they're considering selling their ATM business. All initial inquiries are completely confidential. There is no obligation to move forward at any stage.
