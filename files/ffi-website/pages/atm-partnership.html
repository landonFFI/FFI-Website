Program Details

What Is the ATM Partnership Program?

Ford Frontier Investments purchases and manages the ATM equipment, while you take responsibility for loading the cash. In exchange for that one operational role, you receive 50% of the surcharge revenue from every transaction — our highest revenue share.

You don't buy equipment, pay setup fees, or handle maintenance. The only thing you manage is making sure the machine has cash in it.

Who Is This Program For?

  • Business owners who want the highest revenue split
  • Operators comfortable managing cash loading on a regular schedule
  • Cash-heavy businesses where loading fits naturally
  • Owners who want a more hands-on partnership

Don't Want to Load Cash?

Ask about our cash loading service. We can arrange professional cash replenishment so you capture the higher revenue split without the loading responsibility.
