What Is Wireless ATM Service?

A wireless ATM uses a cellular SIM card to connect to the transaction processing network — the same way a smartphone connects to the internet. This eliminates the need for a dedicated phone line, a broadband internet connection, or any kind of hardwired network infrastructure.

All of our ATM placements operate wirelessly. Whether it's a free placement, a partnership machine, or an ATM you've purchased from us, wireless connectivity is standard.

Why It Matters

Wireless connectivity opens up placement possibilities that would otherwise be unavailable:

  • Outdoor locations, parking lots, and temporary setups
  • Older buildings without easily accessible phone or ethernet lines
  • Events, festivals, and fairs where hardwire isn't an option
  • Remote locations with limited infrastructure
  • Businesses that don't want to share their internet connection

Already Own an ATM?

If you already own an ATM that's currently running on a phone line or wired connection, we can help you convert it to wireless. We provide the cellular equipment and handle the technical configuration. This can significantly improve your uptime reliability and give you more flexibility in where you place the machine.

Contact us to discuss the wireless upgrade process for your specific ATM model.
