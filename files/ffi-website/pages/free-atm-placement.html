How It Works

What Is Full-Service Free ATM Placement?

Our free ATM placement program is exactly what it sounds like: we place a fully-operational ATM at your business at no cost to you. No equipment purchase, no installation fee, no maintenance cost, and no monthly fee — ever.

Ford Frontier Investments owns and operates the ATM. We handle every aspect of running it — cash management, 24/7 transaction monitoring, compliance, and any repairs needed. In exchange, you receive a portion of the surcharge revenue generated by every transaction at your machine.

It is our most popular service because there is genuinely no downside for the business owner. You get a service your customers appreciate, and you earn passive income from the transactions — with zero effort after installation.

Revenue context: The average ATM surcharge in Alabama is approximately $3.75 per transaction. A moderately busy location processing 200 transactions per month generates $750 in monthly surcharge revenue before the split. We discuss your specific split clearly before installing anything — no surprises.

Revenue

How You Get Paid

When a customer uses the ATM at your location, they pay a surcharge fee for the convenience. That surcharge is split between Ford Frontier Investments and you. The exact split depends on your location, foot traffic, and program terms — we discuss this clearly before we ever install anything.

There are no hidden deductions. You receive a transparent monthly statement showing every transaction and your share of the revenue.
