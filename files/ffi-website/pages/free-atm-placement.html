What Is Full-Service Free ATM Placement?

Our full-service free ATM placement program is exactly what it sounds like: we place a fully-operational ATM at your business at no cost to you. There is no equipment purchase, no installation fee, no maintenance cost, and no monthly fee — ever.

Ford Frontier Investments owns and operates the ATM. We handle every aspect of running it, including cash management, 24/7 transaction monitoring, compliance, and any repairs that may be needed. In exchange, you receive a portion of the surcharge revenue generated by every transaction at your machine.

It is our most popular service because there is genuinely no downside for the business owner. You get a service that your customers appreciate, and you earn passive income from the transactions — with zero effort on your part after we install it.

What We Handle — Everything

When we say we handle everything, we mean it. Here's what's included in our free placement program:

  • ATM equipment (we own and supply the machine)
  • Delivery and professional installation
  • All permits and compliance requirements
  • EMV, ADA, and PCI compliance
  • Cash loading and cash management
  • 24/7 remote monitoring and transaction processing
  • On-call maintenance and repair service
  • Wireless cellular connectivity (no phone line or internet needed)
  • Monthly revenue statements

How You Get Paid

When a customer uses the ATM at your location, they pay a surcharge fee for the convenience. That surcharge is split between Ford Frontier Investments and you. The exact split depends on your location, foot traffic, and program terms — we discuss this clearly before we ever install anything.

There are no hidden deductions. You receive a transparent monthly statement showing every transaction and your share of the revenue.

No Long-Term Contracts

Our free placement agreements are fair and straightforward. We don't believe in locking businesses into unreasonable long-term commitments. If something isn't working for you, we talk about it like professionals.
