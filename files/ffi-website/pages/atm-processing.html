ATM Processing — What It Is

ATM transaction processing is the network infrastructure that connects your ATM to the banking system. When a customer inserts their card, enters their PIN, and requests cash, a processing network communicates with the customer's bank in real time to authorize the withdrawal. Without a processing partner, your ATM simply cannot function.

Ford Frontier Investments provides ATM processing services through two of the most established and reliable processors in the industry: PAI (Payment Alliance International) and DNS (Datalink Networks).

Who Is This For?

  • ATM owners who have an existing machine with no processing or outdated processing
  • Operators switching from a higher-cost processor
  • Businesses that recently purchased an ATM from another source and need setup
  • Anyone looking for a local Alabama contact for their ATM processing relationship

Free Reprogramming

For new processing customers, we provide free over-the-phone reprogramming performed by a qualified ATM technician. Most ATMs can be converted to our processing network without any on-site visit.

Compatible machines include most Hyosung, Genmega, and Triton models. Contact us with your machine's make and model and we'll confirm compatibility.
