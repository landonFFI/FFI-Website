What Is the ATM Partnership Program?

The ATM Partnership Program is designed for business owners who want more involvement in their ATM — and more revenue to show for it. In this arrangement, Ford Frontier Investments purchases and manages the ATM equipment, while you take responsibility for loading the cash.

In exchange for loading the cash and taking on that minor operational role, you receive 50% of the surcharge revenue generated by every transaction. This is a higher revenue share than our fully managed free placement program.

It's a minimal hands-on commitment with no financial risk — you don't buy any equipment, pay any setup fees, or take on any maintenance responsibility. The only thing you handle is making sure the machine has cash in it.

Who Is This Program For?

The ATM Partnership Program is a strong fit for business owners who:

  • Want a higher revenue split than the standard free placement program
  • Are comfortable managing their own cash loading on a regular schedule
  • Have easy access to cash (such as a cash-heavy business)
  • Want to keep a closer eye on their ATM's performance
  • Prefer a more hands-on partnership with their ATM operator
