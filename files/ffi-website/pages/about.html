About Ford Frontier

Alabama's Independent ATM Deployer — born and based in the Heart of Dixie, built around honest service and local relationships.

Who We Are

Ford Frontier Investments is an Independent ATM Deployer (IAD) headquartered in Montgomery and Tuscaloosa, Alabama. We are a locally owned and operated ATM company — not a franchise, not a national corporation, and not a call center. When you work with us, you're working with the actual people running the business.

We install, service, and operate ATMs for businesses across Alabama under several different program structures — from fully managed free placements to ATM sales and processing for independent operators. We also offer merchant services for businesses looking to reduce their credit card processing costs.

What Makes Us Different

There are national ATM companies that operate in Alabama. The difference with Ford Frontier Investments is that we're here — in the same state, often in the same city as the businesses we serve.

That means faster response times when something needs attention. It means we can actually visit a potential placement location before signing an agreement. It means you can call us and talk to the person who installed your ATM.

Our Processing Partners

We process ATM transactions through PAI (Payment Alliance International) and DNS (Datalink Networks) — two of the most established and well-regarded networks in the independent ATM industry. This means your transactions are processed reliably and securely, with the infrastructure of a major network and the local service of a hometown operator.

Authorized Dealer

Ford Frontier Investments is an authorized dealer for both Hyosung (Nautilus Hyosung) and Genmega ATM equipment. These are the two most widely deployed retail ATM brands in the United States. As an authorized dealer, we offer wholesale pricing and can provide full manufacturer support for the equipment we sell.
