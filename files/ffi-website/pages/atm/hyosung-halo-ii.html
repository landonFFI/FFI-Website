Common Questions

Halo II FAQ

What is included with my purchase?
Your Halo II comes brand new from the factory, with full ATM processing setup through all major networks, free nationwide shipping, and a 2-year manufacturer warranty. Factory programming is an optional add-on.
Do I need to use FFI for processing?
No — but if you choose to process with another company, a $500 outside processor fee applies at purchase. If you use Ford Frontier Investments for processing, there is no fee and setup is handled for free.
What cassette size should I choose?
A 1K cassette holds 1,000 notes and works well for most small-to-medium locations. If your machine will see heavy traffic, a 2K or Dual cassette means fewer cash loading trips. We're happy to advise — just give us a call.
How does factory programming work?
When you select factory programming, your ATM arrives pre-configured with your surcharge settings and processing credentials. Without factory programming, one of our technicians will walk you through the setup process over the phone at no additional charge — it typically takes 30–60 minutes.
How does ordering work?
Configure your ATM, add it to your cart, and submit your contact info. No payment is collected online. We'll reach out within 24 hours to confirm your order, collect payment (ACH or card), and send you the necessary processing paperwork.
