The Financial Case

Two Ways the ATM Pays for Your Store

Your Share of Every Transaction

Every time a customer uses the ATM, a surcharge is collected. Under FFI's free ATM placement program, you earn a share of that surcharge — every transaction, every month, automatically. No invoices. No work on your end. It just runs.

For stores with steady foot traffic, that adds up to a meaningful monthly number. We will tell you exactly what to expect before we place the machine — no surprises.

Every Cash Sale Skips the Card Fee

Every time a customer pays by card, your store pays a processing fee. It is a percentage of the sale, plus a fixed amount per transaction. On a small-ticket purchase — a drink, a snack, a lottery ticket — that fee can eat a significant portion of your margin on that sale.

When a customer uses the ATM and pays cash instead, that fee disappears. Completely. The more cash your ATM puts in customers' hands, the more of your own sales margin you keep.

Most ATM companies talk about surcharge revenue. Nobody talks about this part. We do, because it matters more than most store owners realize.
