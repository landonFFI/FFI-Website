About Ford Frontier

Alabama's Independent ATM Deployer — born and based in the Heart of Dixie, built around honest service and real local relationships.

Who We Are

A Birmingham-Based ATM Company Built on Local Service

Ford Frontier Investments is an Independent ATM Deployer (IAD) headquartered in Birmingham, Alabama, serving businesses across Birmingham, Montgomery, Tuscaloosa, and the surrounding markets. We are locally owned and operated — not a franchise, not a national corporation, and not a call center. When you work with us, you're working with the actual people running the business.

We install, service, and operate ATMs for businesses across Alabama under several program structures — from fully managed free placements to ATM sales and processing for independent operators. We also offer merchant services for businesses looking to reduce their credit card processing costs.

What Makes Us Different

Local Where It Counts

There are national ATM companies that operate in Alabama. The difference with Ford Frontier Investments is that we're here — in the same state, often in the same city as the businesses we serve.

That means faster response times when something needs attention. It means we can actually visit a potential placement location before signing an agreement. It means you can call us and talk to the person who installed your ATM.

Authorized Dealer

Hyosung & Genmega Authorized Dealer

Ford Frontier Investments is an authorized dealer for both Hyosung (Nautilus Hyosung) and Genmega ATM equipment — the two most widely deployed retail ATM brands in the United States. As an authorized dealer, we offer competitive wholesale pricing and full manufacturer support for the equipment we sell.

Processing Partners

Our Processing Networks

We process ATM transactions through PAI (Payment Alliance International) and DNS (Datalink Networks) — two of the most established and well-regarded networks in the independent ATM industry. Your transactions are processed reliably and securely with the infrastructure of a major network and the local service of a hometown operator.

Our Founder

Why Landon Started FFI

Landon Ford started Ford Frontier Investments with a straightforward observation: Alabama's small and mid-size businesses were being underserved by the national ATM companies operating in the state. Response times were slow, local knowledge was nonexistent, and business owners were locked into arrangements they didn't fully understand.

The goal was simple — build a locally-operated ATM company that treats Alabama businesses the way a neighbor would. Show up when something needs attention. Explain the terms before anything is signed. Share revenue honestly and transparently. Be reachable when you call.

That's still the model. Ford Frontier Investments isn't a franchise or a remote operator. It's an Alabama company, run by Alabamians, for Alabama businesses.
