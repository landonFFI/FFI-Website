How It Works

What Is Wireless ATM Connectivity?

A wireless ATM uses a dedicated cellular SIM card to connect to the transaction processing network — the same way a smartphone connects to the internet. This eliminates the need for a phone line, a broadband internet connection, or any shared network infrastructure.

Every ATM we place, sell, and service operates on wireless cellular connectivity through DPL Wireless — the ATM industry's leading cellular provider. DPL Wireless's Hercules platform delivers dedicated ATM connectivity with a reported 0.5% failure rate, compared to the 4.5% industry average. That's roughly a 9x reliability advantage over conventional connectivity methods.

Why It Matters

Why Wireless Beats Phone Line & Shared Internet

  • Phone lines are unreliable and being discontinued. Telecom carriers are phasing out POTS (plain old telephone service) nationwide. ATMs on phone lines face growing connectivity issues.
  • Shared internet creates risk. Connecting an ATM to your business router is a PCI compliance concern and creates uptime dependency on your ISP.
  • Cellular is purpose-built for ATMs. DPL Wireless is dedicated ATM connectivity infrastructure — not repurposed consumer cellular. It's monitored, managed, and optimized for transaction processing.
Where It Opens Doors

Placement Possibilities That Wired ATMs Can't Reach

  • Outdoor locations, parking lots, and temporary setups
  • Older buildings without accessible phone or ethernet
  • Events, festivals, and fairs where hardwire isn't an option
  • Remote locations with limited infrastructure
  • Businesses that can't or won't share their internet connection

FAQ

Wireless ATM Questions

Does the ATM need to be near my router?

No. The ATM uses its own cellular SIM — it has no dependency on your business Wi-Fi or internet router. Place it wherever it gets the best foot traffic.

Can I convert my existing wired ATM to wireless?

Yes. We convert most Hyosung, Genmega, and Triton models. We provide the cellular equipment and handle the configuration — usually without an on-site visit.

What happens if cellular signal is weak at my location?

We confirm connectivity before deployment. DPL Wireless uses multi-carrier cellular infrastructure and signal boosters where needed to ensure reliable coverage.

Is wireless more expensive than a phone line?

Not with us. Cellular connectivity is included in all our placement and processing programs. We don't charge separately for wireless — it's standard on every ATM we operate or sell.
