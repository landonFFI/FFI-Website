We Buy ATM Routes in Alabama

If you own ATM locations in Alabama and are looking to sell — whether you're retiring, scaling back, relocating, or simply ready to move on — Ford Frontier Investments is an active buyer of ATM portfolios in our market.

We buy all sizes of ATM portfolios. There's no minimum number of machines required. Whether you're selling one ATM location or a network of twenty, we'll evaluate what you have and make a fair, straightforward offer.

What We Typically Look For

  • ATMs located in Alabama (our primary acquisition market)
  • Machines with active location agreements in place
  • Consistent transaction history (though lower-volume locations are still considered)
  • Hyosung and Genmega equipment preferred — other brands evaluated case by case
  • EMV-compliant machines in working condition

The Process

The acquisition process is designed to be as straightforward as possible. There are no obligations until you accept an offer, and we don't pressure anyone into a deal that doesn't make sense for them.

  • Step 1: Contact us with your portfolio details (number of machines, locations, monthly transaction volume)
  • Step 2: We review the information and schedule a call to discuss further
  • Step 3: We make a fair written offer based on transaction history and location quality
  • Step 4: If you accept, we handle all the transition details — equipment transfer, location agreement assignments, and processing changeover

Confidential Inquiries Welcome

We understand that not everyone wants to publicize that they're considering selling their ATM business. All initial inquiries are completely confidential. There is no obligation to move forward at any stage of the process.
