ATM Cash Loading — What We Offer

If you own or operate an ATM in Alabama and need someone to handle cash replenishment, Ford Frontier Investments offers professional cash loading services across our service area.

We work with ATM operators who bought their own machines, business owners in our partnership program who need occasional loading help, and any operator who simply wants the peace of mind of having a reliable local loader.

How It Works

  • We confirm your ATM's location and loading requirements
  • We set up a cash loading schedule that fits your transaction volume
  • Our insured loader visits on schedule and fills your machine
  • You receive confirmation of each loading visit
  • We alert you if the machine reaches low-cash thresholds between scheduled visits

Pricing

Our cash loading service is priced at $1 per transaction processed during the period we're actively loading your machine. This is a simple, transparent pricing model with no hidden fees.

For high-volume locations, armored carrier cash replenishment services may be available. Contact us to discuss your volume and we'll recommend the right solution.

Don't Own an ATM Yet?

Our free ATM placement program handles all cash loading for you at no charge. If you're looking to get an ATM without the responsibility of loading it, that's your best option.
