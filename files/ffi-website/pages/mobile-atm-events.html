ATMs for Events — Why It Matters

Cash is still king at many events — food vendors, ride operators, merchandise sellers, and many other vendors prefer or require cash. When event-goers can't find an ATM, they leave earlier, spend less, or don't come back. A well-placed ATM solves that problem.

Ford Frontier Investments provides fully wireless, mobile ATMs for short-term event deployments across Alabama. We handle delivery, setup, and all technical support so you can focus on running your event.

  • Sporting events and tournaments
  • Concerts and outdoor shows
  • Trade shows and expos
  • Fundraisers and charity events
  • Community events and block parties
  • Private events with large guest counts

How Event ATM Rentals Work

Contact us with your event details — date, location, expected attendance, and duration. We'll confirm availability and provide a quote. On the day of your event, we deliver and set up the ATM. All you provide is access to the deployment area.

We monitor the machine throughout your event and respond quickly to any issues. At the end, we collect the equipment. Simple as that.

Book Early

Event ATM availability is limited, especially during peak festival season in Alabama. We recommend reaching out at least 2–4 weeks before your event date to confirm a unit.
