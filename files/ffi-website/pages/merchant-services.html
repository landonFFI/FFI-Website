Merchant Services for Alabama Businesses

Credit card processing is a necessary cost for most businesses — but that doesn't mean you should be paying more than you have to. Many business owners sign up for merchant services without fully understanding what they're being charged, and processor statements are notoriously difficult to read.

Ford Frontier Investments offers merchant services through our Rate Tracker program. We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing — you should know exactly what you're paying and why.

The Rate Tracker Program

Rate Tracker is our approach to merchant services: we review your current processing statement, identify where you're being overcharged, and offer you a competitive alternative with clear pricing.

We work with Alabama businesses of all types and sizes — retail, restaurants, service businesses, and multi-location operators. If you accept credit cards, we can likely save you money or at minimum give you better visibility into what you're paying.
